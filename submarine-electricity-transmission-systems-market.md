<p><p>Submarine electricity transmission systems utilize various cable types to efficiently transport power undersea. Mass-impregnated cables use a solid insulation made of impregnated paper, ideal for high-voltage applications. Self-contained fluid-filled cables feature a pressurized fluid for insulation and cooling, providing excellent thermal management. Extruded insulation cables utilize plastic materials for insulation, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Other market types may include advanced hybrid cables combining different technologies, catering to diverse application needs and specific environmental conditions.</p></p>
