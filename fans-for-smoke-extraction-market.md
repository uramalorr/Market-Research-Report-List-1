<p><strong>The Fans for Smoke Extraction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Smoke Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Smoke Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke extraction market primarily includes two types of fans: axial smoke fans and centrifugal smoke fans. Axial smoke fans work by drawing smoke through a series of blades, providing efficient flow for low-pressure applications. They are ideal for large spaces like warehouses. Centrifugal smoke fans, on the other hand, utilize a rotating impeller to create higher pressure, making them suitable for ducted systems and longer distances. Both types are essential for effective smoke control in buildings during emergencies.</p></p>
