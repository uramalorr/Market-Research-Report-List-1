<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) controllers facilitate the delivery of electrical power alongside data through Ethernet cables. There are two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and Powered Device (PD) controllers. PSE controllers manage the power supply, ensuring the correct voltage and current are delivered to connected devices, while PD controllers are embedded in devices that receive power, regulating power consumption and ensuring efficient operation. Both types contribute to the growth of smart technologies and interconnected systems.</p></p>
