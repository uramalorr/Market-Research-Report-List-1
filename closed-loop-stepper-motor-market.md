<p><p>The closed-loop stepper motor market encompasses three main types: Permanent Magnet, Hybrid, and Variable Reluctance. Permanent Magnet stepper motors use magnets for light torque and precise positioning, making them suitable for low-power applications. Hybrid stepper motors combine features of both permanent magnet and variable reluctance designs, offering improved performance and efficiency for a wide range of applications. Variable Reluctance motors rely on the magnetic reluctance principle, providing high precision and speed, typically in specialized applications. Each type has distinct advantages tailored to specific requirements.</p></p>
