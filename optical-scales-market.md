<p><strong>The Optical Scales Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Optical Scales</li><li>Absolute Optical Scales</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical scales market is divided into two main types: incremental optical scales and absolute optical scales. Incremental optical scales provide position data by measuring changes in position from a defined reference point, commonly used in applications where relative positioning is sufficient. In contrast, absolute optical scales deliver precise position information at any point in the measurement range, ensuring accurate location tracking without the need for a reference position, making them ideal for high-precision applications in automation and manufacturing.</p></p>
