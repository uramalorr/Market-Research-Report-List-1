<p><p>TAED (Tetraacetylethylenediamine) is a bleach activator widely used in laundry detergents and cleaning products. Its compatibility with cold water washing has made it a popular choice in modern detergents, aligning with the growing demand for sustainable and efficient cleaning solutions.</p><p>The TAED Bleach Activator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period.
