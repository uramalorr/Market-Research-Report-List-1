<p><p>The Smart Window Film market is segmented into two main types: Self-Adhesive Film and Non-Adhesive Film. Self-Adhesive Films are designed for easy application and removal, allowing users to apply the film directly onto existing glass surfaces without additional adhesives. In contrast, Non-Adhesive Films require a different installation method, often involving bonding materials to adhere securely to windows. Both types offer energy efficiency, privacy control, and glare reduction, catering to diverse consumer needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
