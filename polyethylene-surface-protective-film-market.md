<p><p>The Polyethylene Surface Protective Film market comprises three main types: low-density polyethylene (LDPE), medium-density polyethylene (MDPE), and high-density polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE films are flexible and suitable for delicate surfaces, providing excellent conformability and protection. MDPE offers a balance of strength and flexibility, making it ideal for varied applications. HDPE is known for its durability and resistance to punctures and tears, best suited for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific protective needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
