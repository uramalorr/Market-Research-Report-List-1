<p><p>The Middle Voltage Circuit Breakers market includes three primary types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breakers (ACB). MCBs protect against overloads and short circuits in low-voltage applications, while MCCBs offer adjustable trip settings for medium-voltage systems, providing greater flexibility and protection. ACBs, typically used in high-voltage power distribution, are designed for larger loads and can interrupt high currents quickly. Each type serves distinct roles in electrical protection and management within various industries.</p></p>
