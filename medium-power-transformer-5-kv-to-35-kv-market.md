<p><p>The medium power transformer market, covering voltage ranges of 5 kV to 35 kV, includes dry type transformers and oil immersed transformers. Dry type transformers, which use air as a cooling medium, are favored for their safety and low maintenance requirements, making them suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, oil immersed transformers utilize oil for cooling and insulation, offering higher efficiency and capacity for larger power needs, typically deployed in outdoor and industrial settings. Both types serve critical roles in power distribution.</p></p>
