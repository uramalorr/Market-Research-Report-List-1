<p><p>Boat anchor types vary based on design and application. Plow boat anchors, with their curved shapes, effectively penetrate seabeds for strong hold. Flat boat anchors offer a lightweight option, ideal for small boats in calmer waters. Grapnel boat anchors, featuring multiple prongs, are useful for retrieving lost items or securing small crafts. Floating boat anchors, designed for drift fishing, provide stability without sinking. Other market options include specialized anchors like mushroom or claw types, catering to unique boating needs.</p></p>
