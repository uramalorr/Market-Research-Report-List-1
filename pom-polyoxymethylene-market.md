<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) is a high-performance engineering plastic, available in two primary market types: POM-H and POM-C. POM-H, or homopolymer, offers superior mechanical properties and stiffness, making it suitable for precision applications like gears and bearings. Conversely, POM-C, or copolymer, provides enhanced chemical resistance and better impact strength, making it ideal for applications in automotive and consumer goods. The choice between POM-H and POM-C depends on specific requirements like durability, processing ease, and environmental resistance.</p></p>
