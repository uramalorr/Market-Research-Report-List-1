<p><p>The motor grader market is primarily segmented into rigid frame and articulated frame types. Rigid frame motor graders feature a solid chassis, offering stability and precision for tasks like road leveling and grading. They are ideal for flat terrains. In contrast, articulated frame motor graders have a pivot joint between the front and rear sections, enhancing maneuverability and adaptability on uneven landscapes. This flexibility allows operators to navigate tight spaces, making them suitable for various construction and maintenance applications.</p></p>
