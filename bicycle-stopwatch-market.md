<p><p>The bicycle stopwatch market includes three main types: wired computers, wireless computers, and wireless GPS computers. Wireless computers eliminate cables, offering flexibility and ease of installation, though they can face signal interference. Wireless GPS computers enhance functionality by incorporating global positioning technology, allowing cyclists to track distance, speed, and route maps, offering advanced navigation alongside basic performance metrics.</p></p>
