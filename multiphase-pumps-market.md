<p><p>The multiphase pumps market encompasses various types, primarily including twin screw and helico-axial multiphase pumps. Twin screw multiphase pumps utilize two intermeshing screws to efficiently transport a mixture of gas, liquid, and solids, making them suitable for various applications. Helico-axial multiphase pumps employ a helical impeller design to handle large volumes with low shear forces, ideal for delicate fluids. Other market types may include progressive cavity pumps and centrifugal multiphase pumps, addressing diverse industry needs for fluid movement.</p></p>
