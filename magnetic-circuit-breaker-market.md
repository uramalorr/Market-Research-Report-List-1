<p><p>The magnetic circuit breaker market is primarily segmented into two types: single-pole and multi-pole magnetic circuit breakers. Single-pole breakers are designed to protect a single electrical circuit, providing overcurrent protection and ensuring safe operation in residential and light commercial applications. Multi-pole breakers, on the other hand, can manage multiple circuits simultaneously, making them suitable for industrial settings where higher power distribution and protection are required. Both types enhance safety by interrupting current flow during overload or fault conditions.</p></p>
