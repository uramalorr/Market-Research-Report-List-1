<p><p>Intercoms can be categorized into two primary market types: wired and wireless. Wired intercom systems utilize physical cables for communication, providing a stable and secure connection, often suited for permanent installations in residential or commercial buildings. In contrast, wireless intercoms operate through radio frequencies or Wi-Fi, offering flexibility and ease of installation, ideal for temporary setups or locations where cabling is impractical. Each type serves distinct user needs and preferences, affecting range, reliability, and installation complexity.</p></p>
