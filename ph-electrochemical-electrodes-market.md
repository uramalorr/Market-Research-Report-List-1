<p><p>The pH electrochemical electrodes market comprises various types, including pH, ORP, reference, conductivity, and dissolved oxygen electrodes, each serving distinct purposes in analytical and industrial applications. pH electrodes measure acidity or alkalinity, ORP electrodes assess oxidation-reduction potentials, and reference electrodes provide stable potential for measurements. Conductivity electrodes determine ion concentration in solutions, while dissolved oxygen electrodes gauge oxygen levels in water. Together, these sensors enable precise monitoring and control in chemical, environmental, and laboratory settings.</p></p>
