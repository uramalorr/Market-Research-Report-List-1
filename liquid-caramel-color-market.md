<p><p>The Liquid Caramel Color market includes four main types: Plain Caramel Color, made by heating carbohydrates, used in beverages and baked goods. Caustic Sulfite Caramel Color, produced with sulfite compounds, is ideal for acidic drinks. Ammonia Caramel Color, created using ammonia, is common in beer and soy sauce. Ammonia Sulfite Caramel Color, a blend of ammonia and sulfite, is used in soft drinks and confectionery. Each type caters to specific applications based on stability, solubility, and compatibility with different pH levels and food matrices.</p></p>
